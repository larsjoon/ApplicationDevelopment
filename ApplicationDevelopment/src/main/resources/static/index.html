Oostvaardersplassen: A Rewilding Crisis?

The Oostvaardersplassen is a "rewilding" project aimed at creating a self-sustaining ecosystem. However, the rapid growth of large herbivore populations led to mass starvation and public outcry. This simulation models these dynamics to explore sustainable long-term solutions.
